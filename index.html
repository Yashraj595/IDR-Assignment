About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in high-consequence environments.

IDR connects academic insight and real-world projects through collaboration with universities and industry partners.

Our Model

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing AI governance models and digital risk frameworks.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Community

IDR supports students, early-career professionals and practitioners in financial services and critical infrastructure who want to build expertise in digital and cyber risk.
