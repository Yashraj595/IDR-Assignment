Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) logo is designed using a geometric hexagon shape inspired by a cube structure to represent digital systems, resilience, and risk frameworks. The orange color symbolizes innovation, energy, and forward thinking, while black and white provide strong contrast and professionalism suitable for a technology institute. The typography is kept clean and bold to ensure readability across desktop, mobile, and favicon sizes. The design supports two variants: icon-only for compact spaces and icon with text for full branding.
